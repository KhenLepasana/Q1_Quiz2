Spider-Man: Across the Spider-Verse

About the Movie:

Spider-Man: Across the Spider-Verse" is a sequel to the first movie,
"Spider-Man: Into the Spider-Verse.". The main character is Miles Morales, who is Spider-Man in his universe.
The movie continues the story of his journey as Spider-Man, where he encounters various Spider-People and his friends from different dimensions. He faces many challenges throughout the story, including a new villain called The Spot, as he travels through different dimensions.
